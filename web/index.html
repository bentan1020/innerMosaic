Inner Mosiac

Take the first step towards a positive change with Inner Mosiac. Our innovative app uses AI to assist you in effortlessly recording your daily mood and emotions. This intuitive tool is designed to help you gain valuable insights into your emotional well-being, paving the way for a more balanced and fulfilling life.

Tailored Reflection

When engaging with Inner Mosaic you are embarking on a personalized journey towards self-discovery. Our AI-powered system is designed to enhance your experience by intuitively prompting thought-provoking questions based on your entries. These tailored inquiries serve as catalysts for deeper reflection and understanding, guiding you toward a more profound connection with your emotions.

Effortless Digitization

Celebrate simplicity with Inner Mosaic’s innovative journal scanning feature. Now you can effortlessly scan and digitize your handwritten entries making it more convenient and accessible than ever before. Experience the best of both worlds, easily scan and preserve your handwritten journals, but also directly input new entries through our user-friendly interface. Our app seamlessly accommodates your individual style, ensuring that your journey toward self-discovery is as effortless and customizable as possible.

The Mosaics

Experience your emotional journey come to life with Inner Mosaic’s unique visual representation feature. This intuitive visualization provides a comprehensive overview of your well-being, allowing you to see patterns, shifts, and progress in a glance. The vibrant spectrum within the circle paints a vivid picture of your emotional landscape, turning your inner world into a tangible, insightful map for personal growth and self-awareness.

Color Meaning

Colors have an extraordinary ability to communicate and evoke emotions, forming an intricate link between our visual perception and inner feelings.

Hapiness

Gold, with its radiant hue, symbolizes joy and contentment, casting a warm glow on moments of happiness and fulfillment.

Sadness

Shade of blue represents senses of melancholy, reflecting moments of introspection and quiet sadness."

Anger

The passionate and bold red color represents the fiery emotion of anger, signifying intense and often tumultuous feelings.

Confusion

The subdued tone of grey mirrors the feeling of confusion, enveloping moments of uncertainty in a muted, ambiguous hue.

Envy

Shade of green carries the weight of envy, mirroring the longing and desire that can accompany feelings of covetousness and comparison."

Calmness

A clear, serene presence reflects the tranquility and calmness that soothes the mind and heart, providing a peaceful respite from life's storms.

Fear

The deep hue of purple can mirror the unsettling emotion of fear, expressing a sense of apprehension and uncertainty.

The Vision

To empower individuals to take charge of their emotional well-being. By utilizing AI technology, we aim to provide tailored journaling experiences. Additionally, experience a visual representation of your emotional journey through a vibrant mood circle, providing you with a comprehensive overview of your personal progress.
